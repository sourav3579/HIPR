What is Maternity cover?

Maternity coverage, often known as a maternity health insurance policy, is specifically designed for women's health needs. Typically offered as a rider to standard health insurance and mediclaim policies, it covers childbirth expenses. This includes prenatal care, delivery costs (normal/cesarean), and pregnancy-related complications. Some insurers also cover pre- and post-natal expenses. However, it's important to note that these policies usually don't cover pre-existing diseases affecting pregnancy, congenital diseases, or infertility treatment expenses.
